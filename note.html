About My Journey

A little bit about my background :-

So I am Apoorv Sharma. I am basically from Uttarakhand but live in Bangalore as I have completed my studies in Bangalore in Bsc Biotechnology, zoology, and chemistry.

My coding Journey so far :-

In my 3rd year of college, while surfing the internet I saw a very visually appealing website and always felt that the person who must have made this website have such a cool job.

That was the day when I got to know about "frontend development" and I decided that I have to master this. I instantly got a very strong feeling that this is what I want to do and there I was purchasing a brand new copy and putting the very first heading "WHAT IS HTML?".Fast forwarding to today, I have basic to intermediate knowledge of HTML, CSS, javascript, react, and react toolkit, and always looking for opportunities to expand my knowledge.

Though my coding journey is not very smooth as I was all by myself, from searching roadmaps, gathering notes, following the correct technologies, clearing my own doubts through countless google searches, and dealing with frustrating bugs, everything I have managed on my own. There have been times when I almost gave up on coding but in the end this random quote that I found on social media kept me going:-

DO NOT GIVE UP ON SOMETHING THAT YOU CAN'T GO A DAY WITHOUT THINKING ABOUT !

My only motive to learn frontend/full stack development is to gain maximum knowledge to master this field. The quickest way to grow the knowledge exponentially is to learn from industry experts.

I have a never give up attitude and a curious mindset that always searches for answers to expand my knowledge. Being a neoG level one student will raise my confidence to up to multiple folds and I am ready to give my 100% hard work and efforts to NeoG camp if I get selected.
